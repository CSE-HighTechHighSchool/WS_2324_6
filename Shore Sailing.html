Shore Sailing

Setting Sail for Success

Shore Sailing is a not for profit 501(c)(3) organization founded by Jersey Shore students who love sailing to open the world of sailing to those who may not otherwise have the opportunity to enjoy this lifelong activity. Donations are tax deductible and 100% of all donations go back to the community.
