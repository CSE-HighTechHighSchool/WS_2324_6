About Us

We love to sail and founded this organization to bring the joys of sailing free of charge to those who may not otherwise have the chance to “learn the ropes.” Shore Sailing brings camaraderie, sailing education, and sailing opportunities to our friends at local Jersey Shore schools.

Sophia Behar

Founder


Hi, my name is Sophia Behar, and I am the co-founder of Shore Sailing. My journey with sailing started at the age of 7, and it quickly became an all-consuming passion. Having called the Jersey Shore my home throughout my life, my earliest volunteer work for the Jersey Shore involved selling sand crabs to support breast cancer awareness. I have always wanted to shed light on the enjoyment of sailing and what could be a more fitting path than combining my interests: sailing, the Jersey shore, and volunteering to create awareness. In my journey to develop this idea, I've made it my mission to shine a brighter spotlight on the world of sailing, especially among those who may lack the means to support young sailors. With strong dedication, I aspire to bring the joys of sailing to children who may not have the resources to support their dreams, ultimately forging a stronger bond between our community and the adventures on the Jersey shores through sailing.
